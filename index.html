About Us

It started with two friends, an ape and an idea. One being heavily involved in the traditional art market and world renowned museums and the other a professional artist and illustrator full time, both are completely obsessed with crypto and the NFT Space.

The Gardener being a BAYC (Mutant) holder and Archer Mccall being a huge fan of the project from inception, asked each other one question. Where are the ladies? From that ‘aha’ moment the two of them got stuck into creating a female version to the legendary ape.

In order for the project to be a success the team required a woman’s touch and influence. Although the nft market has grown and a number of women are successfully carving out a niche, the trend of male dominance has also become more apparent. The two made it their mission to recruit a team of talented women with various skill sets to take on this project.

What was essential from the beginning is that it will not be another copy and paste derivative in any way. From the character to each trait, every element had to be conceptualized, handsketched and original in its own unique way with some references to female icons.

What is apparent is that the original concept has evolved into something much larger. It is said that the brands and companies of today that have no strategy to enter the NFT space are like those back in the late 1990’s that did not have strategies to get involved as early adopters to the internet.

Our goal is to bridge the NFT space with the physical world by working with people, brands and companies. Collaborating and designing custom ApeWives which can then be used on various applications, one for example as the face/ supermodel for a specific line of cosmetic products. This will enable businesses to enter the NFT space not only by investing in an NFT and storing it in their wallet, but by capitalizing on it and benefiting from it.

The DAW community will then benefit from this strategy as it will offer our members exclusive access to various tangible and virtual experiences. From discounts at exclusive hotels around the world, access to high end clubs, restaurants and even airline upgrade priority is on the list.

Our vision is to create a functional, sustainable and solid membership network so that our community will enjoy the perks of being part of this Exclusive and Unique Club. Last but not least, our roadmap development and activities are first and foremost promoting women, their strong character and leadership capabilities in this new Renaissance. The aim is to address the lack of female representation in Cryptospace. Our team at the Desperate ApeWives celebrates women and looks to collaborate with and promote female NFT artists and entrepreneurs.
